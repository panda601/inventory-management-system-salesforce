## Core Modules & Objects
* **Products (`Product__c`)**: Tracks items, SKUs, cost price, selling price, and stock levels.
* **Suppliers (`Supplier__c`)**: Tracks product suppliers and their contact information.
* **Purchase Orders (`Purchase_Order__c`) & Items (`Purchase_Order_Item__c`)**: Manages inventory procurement.
* **Sales Orders (`Sales_Order__c`)**: Logs customer orders and sales revenue.
* **Inventory Transactions (`Inventory_Transaction__c`)**: Records all stock movements (Inflow, Outflow, Adjustment).
