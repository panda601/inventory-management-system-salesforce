## Principles
1. **Clicks over Code**: Always prefer standard declarative Salesforce features (App Builder, Flows, Validation Rules, Approval Processes, Duplicate Rules) over Apex and Visualforce/LWC where possible.
2. **Security-First Design**: Ensure appropriate Profiles, Permission Sets, OWD settings, and Sharing Rules are defined and strictly adhered to.
3. **Data Quality**: Ensure validations and duplicates are caught early in the lifecycle.
4. **Automation Best Practices**: Limit the number of record-triggered flows per object, use sub-flows or clear entry criteria, and design for bulkification.
